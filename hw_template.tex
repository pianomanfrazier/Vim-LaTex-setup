Here is some pseudocode:
\begin{algorithm}
\caption{Recursive Fibbonacci}
\begin{algorithmic}
\REQUIRE{n}
\ENSURE{the n\textsuperscript{th} fibbonacci number}
	\IF{$n \leq 2$}
		\RETURN{1}	
	\ENDIF
	\RETURN{fibb(n-1) + fibb(n-2)}
\end{algorithmic}
\end{algorithm}
\\
The complexity of the algorithm is $\mathcal{O}(2^n)$.
